Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our algorithmic trading platform

What is algo trading?

Algo trading uses automated systems to execute trades based on predefined strategies, removing human emotions and enhancing efficiency. Our algorithms analyze market conditions 24/7 and make decisions based on statistical patterns and technical indicators.

How do I start using House of Algo's?

Simply sign up, install MT4, and connect to our strategies. Our system will handle the trades automatically. Our team will guide you through the setup process for a smooth onboarding experience.

Do I need trading experience?

No, our automated strategies are designed for both beginners and experienced traders. The algorithms handle all trading decisions, so prior trading knowledge is not required.

What is the minimum investment required?

You can invest a minimum of $100. For higher investments, multiples of $1000 apply (e.g., $2000, $4000, etc.). Different investment levels may grant access to various strategy tiers.

What are the expected returns and risks?

Returns range between 2% to 5% monthly (after a 30% company share), with a highest drawdown of 30% (53% in extreme backtesting). All trading involves risk; past performance is not indicative of future results.

Is there a locking period?

No, you can stop copy trading anytime and withdraw your funds immediately. We provide full flexibility with no lock-in periods or hidden restrictions.

What is the account opening procedure?

A link will be sent to you. Fill in the required details (e.g., Aadhar Card, Driving License, Bank Statement, Email ID, Mobile Number) and your account will be opened within 24 hours with our team’s support.
