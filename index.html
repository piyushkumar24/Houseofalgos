What is algo trading?

A: Algo trading uses automated systems to execute trades based on pre‑defined strategies, removing human emotions and enhancing efficiency.

Q: How do I start using House of Algo's?

A: Simply sign up, install MT4, and connect to our strategies. Our system will handle the trades automatically.

Q: Do I need trading experience?

A: No, our automated strategies are designed for both beginners and experienced traders.

Q: What is the minimum investment required?

A: You can invest a minimum of $100. For higher investments, multiples of $1000 apply (e.g., $2000, $4000, etc.).

Q: What are the expected returns and risks?

A: Returns are 2% to 5% monthly (after a 30% company share). The highest drawdown so far is 30% (53% in extreme backtesting conditions).

Q: Is there a locking period?

A: No, you can stop copy trading anytime and withdraw your funds immediately.

Q: What is the account opening procedure?

A: A link will be sent to you. By clicking it, you must fill in all the details (Aadhar Card, Driving License, Bank Statement, Email ID, Mobile Number). The account will be opened in 24 hours with support from our team through Anydesk.

Q: What is the highest drawdown experienced?

A: Based on backtesting, the highest drawdown has reached 53% under extreme conditions like market crashes or trade wars.

Q: What if there's a server problem?

A: Occasionally, VPS server issues may arise. However, our team is equipped to handle trades manually when needed.

Q: Can tech issues be handled manually?

A: Yes, our expert team can manually manage trades if technical issues arise.
